About Eren Yeager

My head's... Gotten all messed up... I've been thinking every day since I came here. How did things turn out this way? Ruined minds and bodies, people with no freedom left, people who have even lost themselves... people who push themselves into Hell see a different Hell from the rest of us. They also see something beyond that hell. Maybe it's hope. Maybe it's another Hell. I don't know which it is. The only people who do know... are the ones who keep moving forward.
